Functional Training :-

Functional training simply means training your body to perform everyday movements such as lifting bending, climbing stairs. Functional training helps in improving the functional movements. The time spent training with functional equipment in open spaces improves different fitness aspects by developing your agility, flexibility and dynamic strength. This training can be undertaken by anyone from a beginner to an athlete can start training at MultiFit.
